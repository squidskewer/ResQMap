🌋 Earthquake

  • Drop, Cover, Hold On: Get under sturdy furniture, cover head/neck, and hold until shaking stops.
  • Stay away from windows, shelves, and tall furniture.
  • After shaking: evacuate carefully, watch for falling debris.

🌊 Tsunami

  • If you are near the coast and you feel a strong quake → move to higher ground immediately.
  • Stay inland until authorities say it’s safe.
  • Don’t go back for belongings.

🌪 Tornado

  • Go to a basement or small interior room (bathroom, closet) without windows.
  • Cover yourself with a mattress or blankets.
  • If outside: lie flat in a low ditch, not under bridges.

🌀 Hurricane / Typhoon

  • Evacuate if ordered.
  • Stay indoors, away from windows.
  • Have food, water, and batteries for several days.
  • Avoid floodwaters afterward (they may be contaminated or hide hazards).

🔥 Wildfire

  • Leave early - don’t wait until it’s close.
  • Wear cotton clothes (not synthetic).
  • Cover nose/mouth with cloth to reduce smoke inhalation.
  • Drive away with headlights on, not through heavy smoke.

🌊 Flood

  • Move to higher ground immediately.
  • Never walk or drive through floodwaters (“Turn Around, Don’t Drown”).
  • Stay away from downed power lines.

❄️ Blizzard / Extreme Cold

  • Stay indoors if possible.
  • Layer clothing, cover head/hands.
  • Keep emergency heat sources.
  • If in a car, stay inside, run engine occasionally for heat, keep exhaust clear.

🔥 House Fire

  • Crawl low under smoke.
  • Feel doors with back of hand before opening.
  • Get out quickly and don’t go back inside.
  • Call emergency services once outside.

☢️ Chemical Spill / Gas Leak

  • If indoors: seal windows/doors, turn off ventilation.
  • If outdoors: move uphill and upwind.
  • Avoid touching liquids/powders.
  • Follow official evacuation orders.
